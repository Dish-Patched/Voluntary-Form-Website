LifeScape was formed in mid-2014 when Children’s Care Hospital & School and South Dakota Achieve joined together. We are an independent, non-profit organization, serving adults and children in Sioux Falls, South Dakota. Our center in Rapid City provides therapy for children on an outpatient and outreach basis.

About LifeScape Foundation

Whether you are able to give $1 or $1 million, you are making a difference in the lives of over 3,000 individuals throughout South Dakota and the region. Our giving program includes support at all levels, and it all comes from friends like you – it is our foundation. You can support initiatives throughout LifeScape year-round, ranging from investing in state-of-the-art equipment to paying for community activities or helping with rent. You may also choose to offer a legacy gift that will help us in years to come. LifeScape Foundation is governed by a Board of Directors comprised of community, business, and philanthropic leaders who serve voluntarily and without compensation. The Board and Foundation leadership are deeply committed to ensuring your donations are used in a fiscally prudent manner to benefit the children and adults supported by LifeScape.
